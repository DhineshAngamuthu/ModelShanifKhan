ABOUT ME

I love modelling

Shanif Khan

(born 26th of August, 1995) is a self made model and founder of ACARA Modelling Management.
Started off as a young boy with big dreams, now stands as a determined young man with goals.

CAREER AND PERSONAL LIFE:



Shanif Khan graduated college in the year 2016. He got recruited in an IT company but that didn’t last long.
Not longer than two whole days. A little voice inside his head reminded him that his purpose was larger than a desk job.
And so he quit. A year of hardships passed by with no hope for his modelling future.
Once he felt that all hope was lost, he received a life changing offer in the year 2017 from a clothing brand called “Grad by Workaholic”.
This modelling opportunity changed his life and showed him that he was on the right path.
With all this success came a load of misery as well when Shan spiralled into depression and got lost in a dark abyss.
His only friend was the color black that reflected the shade of his heart and mind.
His guardian angel, his mother guided him into the light and showed him that life was worth living.
This period is what Shan considers to be his lowest point in life.
With his mother as his biggest supporter, he began where he left off.
A fiery passion for working out erupted in Shan and he began training day and night.
He found the perfect analogy to turning all the negativity in his life to positive thoughts.
This made him believe that everything happens for a reason and whatever happens, life must go on.

With a desire and deep drive he entered back into the modelling business, Shan took up a part time job at a friend’s clothing store, Tequila.
During this time (2018) is when Shan revived another job opportunity for a company called “Wulf”.
Tackling between a day job and show business was a task for Shan.
Days consisted of running back and forth the store to shoots.
Shan has received warnings from the store manager during several occasions.
The manager had a change of heart when he was invited as a sponsor for the pageant “Mr and Miss Coimbatore 2019”.
Seeing Shanif treated as royalty and looked at as a star changed everything.
Shan’s noticeable humility and adaptable nature is what made him the man he is today.

Mid 2019, national brand named “Demin Hut” approach Shan looking for a new face as they are planning a nation wide launch of their company.
This is major breakthrough for Shan when he decides to chop off his luscious locks and transform into the best version of himself.
This modelling contract changed his life and gave him new insights.

Now, Shan poses as the brand ambassador for a domestic brand “Scoot Clothing” and tee shirt company “F Bros”.

ACHIEVEMENTS:



Shan has rocked the pageant world in and around Coimbatore, creating a name for himself. His well known achievements are :

1. Mr King of Coimbatore (2018)
2. Mr Style Icon of Erode (2018)
3. Mr Fresh Face of Chennai (2018)
4. Best Model of Coimbatore (2019)

FUTURE GOALS:



Shan has achieved a lot until now and is not stopping ever. His hard work and determination to make a place in this world is truly inspirational.
His goal is to become a brand ambassador of an international company and to win the Mr India title.
His long term goal is to start a clothing brand all the while still training young models all over Tamil Nadu and potentially India.
His ambition is truly remarkable.

EARLY LIFE :

Shan was born August 26, 1995 in Palakkad, Kerala.
A miracle baby born with a life threatening fever that made doctors believe he wouldn’t make it, survived.
Shan was raised by a single mother who is his biggest fan and one true hero.
At school he was known as the hyper kid who made everyone laugh.
Shan was athletic and enjoyed anything and everything that made him feel alive.
He enjoyed dancing in his free time and looked up to Michael Jackson.
Even as a child, Shan yearned to be different and always stood out of the crowd.

During the first year of college is when Shan discovered his deep interest in the world of modelling.
Getting a start wasn’t easy, when his friends did not include him in their recreational photoshoots.
The first time he was invited to these unfriendly photoshoots he was teased for his lack of style.
This drove him to figure out his model instincts and led him to grow out his hair which later become the star of the show.

THE BIRTH OF ACARA :



Shan had his fair share of struggles when it comes of succeeding in this industry. He has faced criticism, failure, harassment and been laughed at.
He knows the pain that comes when working in a field of competitors, trolls and predators and doesn’t wish it upon anyone.
He looks at budding models and hopes to help and guide them through the tough times.
This is the reason for finding the first ever modelling company in Coimbatore, “ACARA Modelling Management”.

ACARA found in the year 2019 is an active modelling-training company that provides workshops that educate aspiring models on modelling.
The workshop goers are then taken under the ACARA wing and given adequate training to transform them from amateur￼ models to modelling superstars.
Shan believes in the potential of these young models and moulds them as confident models ready to take over the world.
The only aim of this company is to train models and show them the way to success
